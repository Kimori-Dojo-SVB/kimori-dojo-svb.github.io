Kimori Dojo SVB is the Silicon Valley Branch of Toshiharu Sawada's O-mei Kai Kimori Dojo under Aikikai Hombu Dojo.

Aikido is a traditional Japese martial art whose practitioners develop the skill of neutralizing violence without causing injury. Learn more about aikido using the resources links above.

Kimori Dojo provides adult training 3 days a week at our Sunnyvale location. We do not have kids classes.
