    Trying new things

    Posted on September 11, 2022

    The website went down for about a week, so I needed to get a new one up pretty quick. This is a work in progress, but hopefully I’ll have fun learning a bunch of new things as I make this site work. I’ll be importing a bunch of images and content from the old site in the near future, but I’m happy to have something back up for now. If you want to train with us, please use the Contact Us page or email kimoridojo@gmail.com and we’ll get you on the mat.
